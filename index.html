8 Chance Calculation Logic
How lucky draw chances were calculated — based on T&C clauses 4a–4h

Core Formula (Clause 4f)

Chances = min( floor( Net Deposit ÷ $200 ) , floor( Trading Lots ÷ 2 ) ) , capped at 65

Both conditions must be met simultaneously — this is the tandem requirement. The lower of the two determines the chance count.
